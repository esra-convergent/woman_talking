# Emotion Video Loops

Place your emotion video loops here:

## Required Videos:
- `neutral.mp4` - Calm, neutral expression (your current idle-avatar.mp4)
- `happy.mp4` - Smiling, cheerful, upbeat
- `sad.mp4` - Frowning, downcast, melancholy
- `angry.mp4` - Stern, frustrated expression
- `surprised.mp4` - Wide eyes, raised eyebrows
- `thinking.mp4` - Contemplative, pondering

## Specifications:
- **Duration:** 6-10 seconds (will loop)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 720p or 1080p
- **File size:** <5MB each
- **Lighting:** Consistent across all videos
- **Framing:** Same position/zoom level

## How to Generate:

### Option 1: AI Tools
1. Upload your base video to HeyGen/D-ID
2. Select emotion presets
3. Generate and download

### Option 2: Manual Recording
1. Record yourself showing each emotion
2. Keep same setup/lighting
3. 6-10 second loops
4. Export as MP4

### Option 3: For Testing
Move your current `idle-avatar.mp4` here as `neutral.mp4` and duplicate it for other emotions (we'll replace later).
